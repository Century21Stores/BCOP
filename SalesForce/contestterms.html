WEEKLY ENTRY PERIODS:

Each Show Off Your Savings Contest lasts one week, starting Monday, April 8, 2019, and continuing until Sunday, June 16, 2019. Entries for each week’s contest must be posted to Instagram in the period starting each Monday at 12:00 a.m. Eastern Daylight Time, and ending the following Sunday at 11:59 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time (the “Weekly Entry Period”). The Instagram server is the official time-keeping device for the Contest. Entries posted within one Weekly Entry Period will not be held or rolled over in future Weekly Entry Periods. Odds of winning depend on the number of Submissions received in each Weekly Entry Period.

The winner will be directed to an online form, where he/she will be required to provide his/her name, mailing address, and date of birth to confirm eligibility and for prize fulfillment purposes within 3 days of the date notice or attempted notice is sent in order to claim the prize.
