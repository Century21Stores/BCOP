Century 21 Department Stores Mobile Alerts: SMS Terms and Conditions

What are Century 21 Department Stores C21 Mobile Alerts?

Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts are SMS messages that are sent to opted-in users that contain informational content for Century 21 Department Stores customers. After an initial confirmation SMS message, users will be opted in to a recurring SMS program

How do I sign up for Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts?

Sign up for Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts by texting OPTIN to LUVC21 (588221). By signing up, you will receive up to 10 Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts per month. Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts are not mobile spam. The service is a recurring message program.

Is it free?

Although all Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts are complimentary, Message & Data Rates May Apply. Depending on your text plan, you may be charged by your carrier.

What if I don’t want to receive any more service messages?

To stop receiving all Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts Alert messages, simply text STOP to the short code LUVC21 (588221). Once your telephone number is removed from our system, you will receive a final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent to your cell phone or mobile device.

What if I want to opt back in?

You can start receiving Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts messages again by following any of the steps outlined in the above section: “How do I sign up for Century 21 Department Stores/C21 Mobile Alerts”.

What if I want more info?

To request more info, simply text HELP to the short code LUVC21 (588221) or call 1-877-350-2121 (Mon-Fri 9AM to 5PM EST).
