CENTURY 21 DEPARTMENT STORE: DELIVERING VALUE TO LIVE BETTER

Century 21 Department Store, a NYC icon for more than 55 years, is legendary for its exceptional offering of designer brands at amazing prices. Century 21 remains a leader in high-end off-price fashion retail, offering men's, women's and children's apparel, footwear, outerwear, lingerie and accessories, along with beauty and home goods at select stores and online at C21Stores.com. The retailer is headquartered in Downtown Manhattan and recently opened their 12th store at Sawgrass Mills in Sunrise, Florida. Locations in New York include Lincoln Square, Brooklyn, Queens and Long Island. There is a location in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania as well as three stores in New Jersey: Paramus, Morristown and Elizabeth. For more information, follow Century 21 Department Store on facebook.com/century21stores and @Century21stores on Twitter and Instagram.
